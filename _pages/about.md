I am a Master’s student in Economics at the University of St. Gallen, affiliated with the Chair of Health Economics, Policy and Management at the School of Medicine, where I work as a research assistant.

My research interests are in applied econometrics, with a particular emphasis on health economics and causal inference. I am especially interested in empirical methods for prediction and causal analysis using observational data.

My current work focuses on applied econometric analysis in health-related settings.
