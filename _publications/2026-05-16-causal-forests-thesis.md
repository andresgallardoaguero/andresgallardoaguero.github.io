Master's thesis in progress at the University of St. Gallen, expected completion in early 2027. Simulation study evaluating whether causal forest methods (GRF, MCF) and their variable importance measures correctly recover heterogeneous treatment effects and identify the variables that drive them, using clinical data generating processes with known ground truth.
