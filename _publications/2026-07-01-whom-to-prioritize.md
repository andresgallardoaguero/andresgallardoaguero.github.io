Co-authored project with [Justus Vogel](https://www.unisg.ch/en/university/about-us/organisation/detail/person-id/9acbc1c5-f703-41b5-8fd5-cfb5d2edc825/), [Julian Klug](https://www.julianklug.com/), [Miodrag Filipovic](https://www.h-och.ch/ueber-uns/team/miodrag-filipovic/), and [Urs Pietsch](https://www.h-och.ch/ueber-uns/team/urs-pietsch/) (corresponding author: Justus Vogel). (January 3 to 5, 2027). The full paper is in progress.
